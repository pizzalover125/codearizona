10 hours of fun, hacking, and workshops!

Code Arizona is a ten-hour event where high-school hackers from across Arizona come together to build apps, games, and much more with the power of code. There are workshops to help you get started, mentors, food, and much more to help you build awesome things at Code Arizona.

💡

Build stuff!

By the end, you'll have built something new and unique with your team.

🧠

Learn skills

You'll learn tons of valuable programming skills from workshops and mentors.

🤝

Make connections

Over the course of the hackathon, you'll meet fellow hackers and make something amazing together.

Swag + Prizes!

Frequently Asked Questions

Any other questions? Send us a message ->

Hacking? That sounds scary.

It's coding and tinkering with computers, not "hacking into the mainframe."

I'm not very good at coding...

That's okay! We'll have workshops teaching web, games, and beginner fundamentals.

What should I bring?

Bring a laptop, chargers, and a positive mindset!

Will food be provided?

Yes! Food and snacks are provided throughout the event.

How much does entry cost?

Nothing at all. The event is 100% free for all!

Do I need a team to join?

Nope. You'll have time to form teams (max three per team) or work solo.
